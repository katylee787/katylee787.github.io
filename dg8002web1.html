CLASSIC STUFFED PEPPERS


Measurements Ingredients
4 large bell peppers (any colour)
1 lb lean ground beef
2 tbsp chopped onions
1 cup cooked rice
1 tsp salt
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
15 oz organic tomato sauce
3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese


INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Heat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cut thin slice from stem end of each bell pepper to remove top of pepper
  3. Remove seeds and membranes; rinse peppers. If necessary, cut thin slice from bottom of each pepper so they stand up straight
  4. In 4 quart Dutch oven, add enough water to cover peppers
  5. Heat to boiling; add peppers
  6. Cook about 2 minutes and then drain
  7. In 10-inch skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until beef is brown; drain
  8. Stir in rice, salt, garlic and 1 cup of tomato sauce, cook until hot
  9. Stuff peppers with beef mixture, stand peppers upright in ungreased 8-inch square glass baking dish
  10. Pour remaining tomato sauce over peppers
  11. Cover tightly with foil, bake for 10 minutes. Uncover and bake about 15 minutes longer or until peppers are tender
  12. Sprinkle with cheese, and you're done!

GRANDMA'S APPLE PIE


Measurements Ingredients
1 9inch double crust pie
1/2 cup unsalted butter
3 tbsp all purpose flour
1/4 cup water
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
8 medium apples (peeled, cored and sliced)


INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan, stir in flour to form a paste
  3. Add water, white sugar, brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer
  4. Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly
  5. Cover with a lattice work crust, and gently pour sugar and butter liquid over the crust, pour slowly so that it does not run off
  6. Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven, and reduce the temperature to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  7. Continue baking for 35-45 minutes until apples are soft
  8. Let it cool for 15-20 minutes and enjoy warm apple pie right out the oven!

DELICIOUS HOT COCOA

Measurements Ingredients
2 cups milk
2 tbsp granulated sugar
2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 cup chocolate chips
1/8 cup vanilla extract

Optional:
  • whip cream
  • marshmallows
  • chocolate pieces


INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Place milk, cocoa powder, and sugar in a small saucepan
  2. Heat over medium/medium-low heat, whisking frequently until warm (but not boiling)
  3. Add chocolate chips and whisk constantly until the chocolate chips melt and distribute evenly into the milk
  4. Whisk in vanilla extract, serve immediately for a warm and delicious hot cocoa!
  5. Add optional ingredients such as marshmallows or whip cream as needed
